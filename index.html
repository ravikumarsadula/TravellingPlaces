My Favorite Places to Visit in India

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, this magnificent white marble mausoleum is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Golden Temple

Situated in Amritsar, Punjab, the Golden Temple is one of the most sacred and serene places for Sikhs.

Ladakh

Located in the northernmost region of India, known for its beautiful landscapes, monasteries, and trekking routes.

Andaman Islands

Known for their pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and coral reefs, ideal for water sports and relaxation.

Kerala Backwaters

Explore the tranquil backwaters of Kerala, famous for its scenic boat rides and lush green landscapes.

Munnar

Munnar, in Kerala, is famous for its vast tea plantations and beautiful hill stations.

Varanasi

One of the oldest cities in the world, known for its spiritual significance and ghats along the Ganges river.

Jaipur

The capital of Rajasthan, also known as the "Pink City," with its royal palaces and forts.

Amber Fort

Located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, this historic fort is known for its beautiful architecture and scenic views.

Red Fort

The historic fort located in Delhi, known for its Mughal architecture and significance in India's history.

Ranthambore National Park

This wildlife sanctuary in Rajasthan is one of the best places to spot tigers and other wildlife.
